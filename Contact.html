Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery from our location. Delivery fees vary based on distance. You can place your order by calling us directly.

Do you have vegetarian/vegan options?

Yes, we have several vegetarian and vegan options clearly marked on our menu. We can also accommodate certain dietary restrictions with advance notice.

Are you available for private events?

Yes, we cater private events and can also host events at our location during off-hours. Please contact us with details about your event for pricing and availability.

What safety measures are you taking

We follow all recommended health guidelines, including regular sanitization of surfaces, providing hand sanitizing stations, and ensuring proper ventilation.
